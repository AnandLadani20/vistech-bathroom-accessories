Company Overview

Our company Shree Om Steel was established in 2015 in Rajkot, Gujarat, as a manufacturer and exporter of premium bathroom accessories under the brand name Vistech®.

Led by partners Mr. Vishal Patel and Mr. Yogesh Patel, with Mr. Nayan Patel as Operations Head, we manufacture napkin holders, liquid soap dispensers, soap dishes, towel stands, toothbrush holders, towel rods, towel racks, robe hooks, corner shelves, tissue holders, and kitchen cutlery — all in premium S.S 202 stainless steel.

Due to attractive design, sturdy construction, perfect finish, and corrosion resistance, our range is widely demanded across India. Products are organised in five collections — Kiva (KV), Rella (RE), Bruna (BR), Canata (CA), and Vistech (500 series) — each with clear series numbers and MRP as per our catalogue.

Our Journey

2015

Shree Om Steel founded in Rajkot by Mr. Vishal Patel & Mr. Yogesh Patel — manufacturing bath accessories for the domestic market.

2017

Launched Vistech® brand with Kiva (KV) and Rella (RE) series — establishing series-wise product numbering.

2019

2022

Expanded Vistech 500 series — multi-function shelves, corner units, towel systems & kitchen cutlery line.

2025
